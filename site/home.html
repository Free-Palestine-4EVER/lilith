Questions of the house

What metals and purities do you use?

Solid 18k yellow and rose gold, and 950 platinum. Every serpent is solid throughout, never plated, never hollow.

How is sizing handled?

After you reserve, a client advisor confirms your exact size, by ring gauge or your existing measurements, before we cast.

How long until my serpent arrives?

Each piece is cast and finished to order by a single maker. Expect six to eight weeks from confirmation, shipped fully insured.

What is the open mouth?

The agape jaw transforms the serpent into a working cigarette holder, a LILITH signature, offered complimentary when you choose a gemstone gaze.

How does the preorder work?

Reserve your configuration now to secure your place and price. A client advisor confirms the details, sizing and deposit before any work begins.
